About Me

Hello, my name is Haris Mahmood. I'm a Web+UI designer & developer based in Toronto. I spend the majority of my time creating awesome applications and user interfaces that look and function incredibly well. When I'm not doing this I'm either playing around with my camera, have my face flat against my pillow, or busy day-dreaming about Emma Watson, Isla Nublar and Winterfell.

My projects range from, but are not limited to, merch design for musicians, company branding + identity design, to full scale web applications.

Jodie Potter

Jodie Potter is a U.S. based wedding photographer focusing in portraits for weddings, maternity, newborn babies, seniors, families and engagements. The client preferred to stay away from the traditional and cliche photography-related branding concepts, and opted for something a little more personal.
