Hi, my name is Haris Mahmood. I'm a Web+UI designer & developer based in Toronto. I spend the majority of my time creating awesome user interfaces that look and function incredibly well. When I'm not doing this I'm either playing around with my camera, have my face flat against my pillow, or busy day-dreaming about Emma Watson, Isla Nublar and Winterfell.

My projects range from, but are not limited to, merch design for musicians, company branding + identity design, to full scale web applications. If there's something you need that I can't do, I'll help you get in touch with someone who can!
